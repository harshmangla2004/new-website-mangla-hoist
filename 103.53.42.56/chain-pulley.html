Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a Chain Pulley Block?

    • Chain Pulley Block (also known as a chain hoist or manual chain hoist) is a manually operated hoist which is used to lift and lower heavy material using chain and pulley. A chain is wound around two wheels which, when pulled, it winds around the wheel and begins to lift the load, and when loosened, it lowers the item. The load is attached via hook.

  • How to operate a chain pulley block?

    • Chain pulley block is the easiest of the machine to operate. Just connect the load to the grabbing hook, which is connected with the chain/rope. Then pull the chain down to lift the load up, if you want to lower the load, then slowly let go of the chain, and the item will come down. Use lifting slings or chain bag for smooth lifting experience.

  • What are the uses of the chain pulley block?

    • Due to its slim design and easy installation, chain pulley blocks have many applications. It is commonly used in garages, construction sites, assembly-line factories, and workshops. Chain Blocks come in a variety of different capacities, making them suitable for a wide range of operations.
