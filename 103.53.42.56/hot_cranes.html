Mangla HOT Cranes

Mangla Hoists manufacturers **HOT cranes** are hand-operated overhead traveling cranes. Also known as "Manual Overhead Cranes", they are ideal for installing equipment, maintenance work, flameproof area, cement industries, engineering units.

Mangla Hoists is the leading **HOT Crane Manufacturer.** We offer high-quality HOT Crane, which is tested for safety, efficiency, ease of use, and other key parameters.

Product Description

Mangla’s sole objective is to make its client’s experience pleasant and safe. We continuously strive to customize, innovate and improve on manufacturing as well as quality and after-sales service.

Some features of Mangla HOT cranes are:

  • Sturdy Design: Mangla **HOT cranes** solve the company’s everyday heavy lifting load challenges with durable, robust and state-of-the-art designs. Our cranes require low maintenance and last for a long span of time.
  • Top-notch Performance: Mangla **HOT Cranes** are not only designed to serve safe and efficient lifting of heavy material loads but also allows easy access in all directions. Attention to our manufacturing process ensures trouble-free operations.
  • International Quality Standards: Designed and manufactured in accordance with latest IS:3177 / IS:807, Mangla meets all quality norms and standards set by the industry.
  • Custom-Built: For the benefit of the client, Mangla offers cranes availability in various sizes, capacities and technical specifications as per the application requirements. We understand that every industry is different and each client can have various requirements; therefore, we offer different class/duty cranes manufactured as per customized requests.
  • Pre and Post Installation services: Mangla believes in long term association with all its clients. Therefore, along with standard quality cranes, we also offer the best cost-effective, installation, repair, and post-installation services to all our clients. Training the workforce on the crane’s functionality and safety is also a key component of installation services provided by us.

Key Specifications of Mangla HOT Cranes

Bridge IS 807/IS 3177/IS800
End CarriagesBox type in construction & fabricated from the rolled section
L.T. WheelsTwo nos. straight type, En8 forged steel, double flanged LT wheels are provided in each of the two end carriages. These wheels are supported on steel axles. For underslung cranes S.G.I tapered type single flanged wheels are provided.
Pinion/AxleMade from En9, heat-treated carbon alloy steel
Gears IS 4460, made from EN 8 and are supported on ball bearings.
BearingsThe heavy-duty sealed ball bearing is used, thus regular lubrication is eliminated. Two ball bearing is provided in each wheel for smooth running.
HookIS 3815 / IS 15560. Made from forged steel - C20, C30 or equivalent.
Hoist Chain pulley block.
Load ChainGrade 80 alloy steel as per IS 6216.
Hand Chain Grade 30 M.S. chain as per IS 2429.
Testing 25% overload as per IS 3177.
