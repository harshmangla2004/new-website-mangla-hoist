Electric Chain Hoists

Mangla Hoists offers a wide array of Electric Chain Hoists manufactured by Indef. An electric chain pulley is a small and lightweight lifting hoist which simplifies the material lifting and handling needs. In contrast to Indef's P and M series, which uses human power to lift the load, an electric chain hoist leverages electricity to lift the material via an electric motor and transmission mechanism.

Mangla offers different types of electric chain hoists with various load capacities, ranges, and specifications to fulfill all your lifting requirements.

Product Description

Indef’s HC+ series is suitable for a wide range of applications. Its rugged European design allows it to handle the load ranging from 25 kgs to 5000 Kgs.

Electric chain hoists are effectively used in various industries for medium to heavy loads, assisting in speeding up work cycles and safety. Indef HC series are constructed according to a state of the art design and equipped with an overload cut-off to prevent overloads.

Some of the distinct characteristics of this Electric Chain Hoist is:

  • Wide range- highly suitable for varied applications.
  • Rugged European design- assures safety and reliability.
  • Gravity die-cast gearbox body and cover- improves performance and reduces dead weight.
  • Precision machine-cut hardened alloy steel gears and load chain wheel- long life and smooth operation.
  • Easily adjustable upper and lower hook positions- protection against overloading.
  • Imported Grade 80 surface hardened load chain- guarantees wear resistance and chain strength.
  • Hoist brake motor single/dual speed- suitable for extreme hoist duty/inching applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is an Electric Chain Hoist?

    • An electric chain hoist is an easy to use device used for lifting or lowering loads. This hoist’s smooth functioning is made possible with the assistance of an electric motor and controller, which effectively helps to lift or lower and accelerate or decelerate its speed.

      The most unique selling point of electric chain hoists is that they provide true vertical lift, thus the load does not differ from the hoist centreline during hoisting or lowering.

  • Where can we use Electric Hoist?

    • The Electric hoists are ideally suited for use in industrial production lines and small machine shops as they require more frequent and faster lifting.

  • What will be the impact of an electricity failure on an Indef electric chain hoist which is running or in use?

    • Indef electric chain hoists have been designed to take care of all safety measures and therefore, in case of a power outage, it automatically applies brakes and promptly stops operation.

  • Can you explain how to change the speed of an Indef electric chain hoist that comes with dual speed?

    • The design of switches may differ from model to model in different types of Indef electric chain hoists but usually push buttons are provided. Pushing to the first stage will ensure low speed whereas; the second stage will be for a higher stage.

  • How much excess load can an Indef electric chain hoist safely withstand?

    • It is strictly prohibited and will be highly risky to try and run any of the Indef electric chain hoists beyond the mentioned capacity.

  • What are some of the applications of electric chain hoist?

    • Indef's electric chain hoists have a wide range of applications across multiple industries, like factories, storage units, wind-power stations, logistics, ports, construction fields, and other places or workshops.
