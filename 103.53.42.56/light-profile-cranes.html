Light Profile Cranes

Mangla Hoists, a trusted partner of Indef, delivers and installs **Light Profile Crane Systems**, an efficient solution at the workstation level for indoor applications. Indef Light Profile Cranes come in two variants: single girder light crane and double girder light crane. It's a cost-effective solution where the installation of other cranes, like bridge cranes, is ineffective due to the need for a raised runway structure.

A **Light Profile crane** has exceptional load capacity and minimum deadweight, making it a convenient solution for local lifting and transportation problems. Its optimized design results in high uptime, low operational and maintenance costs. Furthermore, we can customize **Light Profile Cranes** as per your business needs.

Product Description

**Indef** manufactures a wide range of advanced **Light Profile Cranes** that are of the highest quality, latest technology, and great value for money.

This **Light Profile Crane System** is user-friendly and widely applied in automobiles and coach factories in assisting to move and lift heavy loads with ease.
