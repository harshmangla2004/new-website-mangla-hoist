Your Questions, Answered

Welcome to the Mangla Hoists FAQ section. We've compiled a list of common questions about our products and services to help you make an informed decision. If you can't find the answer you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact our team of experts directly.

What is the difference between an EOT crane and a JIB crane?

An **EOT (Electric Overhead Traveling) crane** moves along a runway at the top of a building and is used for heavy-duty lifting in large industrial spaces. A **JIB crane**, on the other hand, is mounted to a wall or column and is ideal for localized, repetitive lifting tasks within a specific workstation.

Learn more on our EOT Cranes and JIB Cranes product pages.

How do I choose the right crane or hoist for my business?

Choosing the right equipment depends on several factors, including the maximum load capacity you need to lift, the frequency of use, the span and height of your workspace, and the type of material being handled. Our team of experts at Mangla Hoists can provide a personalized consultation to help you find the perfect solution. Contact us today for assistance.

What are the key safety features of your wire rope hoists?

Our **electric wire rope hoists** are designed with multiple safety features, including an electromagnetic fail-safe brake, limit switches for preventing over-hoisting and over-lowering, and a robust construction that complies with all relevant safety standards.

For more details, visit our Electric Wire Rope Hoist page.

Do you provide installation and maintenance services?

Yes, Mangla Hoists provides end-to-end services, including professional installation, commissioning, and comprehensive maintenance support for all our lifting solutions. Regular maintenance ensures the longevity and safe operation of your equipment. Find out more on our Services page.
