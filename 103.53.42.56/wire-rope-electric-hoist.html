Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a Wire Rope Electric Hoist?

    • A **Wire Rope Electric Hoist** is a small and compact device, electrically geared that is put to use to lift heavy loads with the assistance of a drum, around which the rope is wrapped.

      Somewhat similar in structure and functioning to Chain Rope Hoists, **Wire Rope Electric Hoists** are most widely used. These hoists are preferred over chain ropes as they can lift heavy weights at a comparatively high speed.

  • What is the reason for the popularity of Wire Rope Electric Hoists?

    • **Wire Rope Electric Hoists** are hugely popular because:


      1. They are small, compact, lightweight and handy.

      2. It offers simplified installation along with easy portability.

      3. Best for handling heavy loads at fast speed.

      4. Since electricity is inexpensive and available almost everywhere, it makes them useable

  • What are some main features of Indef Wire Rope Electric Hoists that make it stand out?

    • Mangla Hoists supplies Indef **Wire Rope Electric Hoists** that have distinctive features such as:


      1. It covers a wide capacity range, from 1 ton to 40 tons.

      2. Robust hoist drive system design.

      3. Electric Hoists input power range options from 380V to 415V.

      4. Monorail trolley adjustable for a wide range of beams.

      5. Two-speed motor standard.

  • What are the various types of Indef Wire Rope Electric Hoists?

    • There are three types of Indef **Wire Rope Electric Hoists** available:


      1. Monorail

      2. Single Girder

      3. Double Girder


      The mounting options also come in 3 categories:


      1. Trolley

      2. Suspension

      3. Foot Mounted

    • Yes, being the industry leader, it is easy to cater to all the specific demands of our customers. For any further queries or to know in detail, please feel free to contact us.

  • What are types of cranes Indef Wire Rope Electric Hoists is compatible with?

    • It can be installed on a single beam crane, double beam crane, Gantry Crane, Overhead Crane, linear crane for various industrial needs. **Electric wire rope hoist** is widely used for load handling by industrial and mining enterprises, railways, and warehouses, etc.
