Mangla Goliath Cranes

Mangla Hoists are leading manufacturers of **Goliath cranes** that offer customized solutions for Lifting and handling. Their unique design of **Goliath cranes** makes them cost-effective and practical when it comes to space utilization. These are particularly useful for applications wherein there are no columns available for cranes like outdoors.

With a customer-first approach, we are providing crane systems to most of the industry leaders across all verticals. Due to our vast geographical reach, we are able to deliver our world-class hoisting products and solutions to workshops across the country in a seamless and quick manner.

Features of Mangla Goliath Cranes

  • Compact and Power Efficient Designs: Compact dimensions, low weight, reliable operations, high rigidness, and low maintenance are the key features of Mangla **Goliath Cranes**. In addition to this, they are efficient enough to save power!
  • Safe and Quality Parts: To ensure 100% safety to workers, Mangla Hoists uses and manufactures ISO 9001 certified equipment only. It applies to each level of the supply chain. Along with safety measures, the products are easy to install, training for operations & maintenance as well as inspections and post-sales services.
  • VVVF Drives with Interlocking: Mangla **goliath cranes** offer micro speed for all motions through the inverter (VVVF) drives with suitable interlocking. All crane motions are protected with limit switches to prevent over hoisting & over lowering of hook. Also, to detect faults beforehand, a closed-loop circuit for the hoisting mechanism with encoder feedback is used by us.
  • Heavy-duty Mechanism: Before dispatch, Mangla carries out all testing of hoisting and C.T. motion as per IS:3177 standards. It includes the deflection test in the presence of a third-party inspector.
  • Custom-make:We are the experts when it comes to customizing the cranes, with our attention to details, flawless measurements, and qualified engineers, we understand your business needs and provide you the solution that works for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are some applications of goliath cranes?

    • The **goliath crane** is a perfect fit for the industries which require heavy lifting, maneuvering loads, which are challenging to move around with other equipment. It finds a lot of applications in the shipping industry, automobile industry, steel plants, stone and granite industry, heavy machinery manufacturing industry, freight yards, yard goods, and bulk cargoes, etc. Mangla Hoist is the leading **goliath crane** manufacturers in India that produce specialty cranes for unique applications.

  • What makes goliath cranes so effective and efficient?

    • The all-metal structure and the frame, and slick design allows it to walk on the track of the ground. The ends of the beam have an outrigger cantilever beam. The crane has the characteristics of high site utilization rate, broad operating range, wide adaptability, and strong versatility and has wide application, especially in the port cargo yard.

  • How does goliath cranes ensure safety?

    • With master and slave configurations, the crane is operated by a single operator from one control station. It reduces risk and improves safety and flexibility.
