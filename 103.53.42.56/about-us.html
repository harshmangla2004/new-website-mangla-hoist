About Mangla Hoists

Mangla Hoists is India’s leading business house in Cranes & Hoists.

Providing a wide range of advanced lifting solutions including EOT cranes, Electric Hoists, Gantry cranes, Jib crane and a vast range of tackles.

With its head office in Delhi, the thriving capital of the largest democracy- India, Mangla Hoists has been offering the most innovative industrial cranes and handling solutions since its inception in 1973.


We’ve been in the Lifting Businesses™ for 45+ years, covering a wide spectrum of the industry like Power, Steel, Cement, Paper, Chemicals & Fertilizers, Automotive, Railways, Engineering and many more.
