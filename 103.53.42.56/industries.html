Mangla Hoists Industries

Growing step by step, learning from each individual experience, innovating, customizing and expanding for **45+ years**, Mangla Hoists can today boast as being a global leader in the lifting and material handling industry.

Our comprehensive range of outstanding products caters to a wide array of industries, from automotive to logistics. Being customer-centric has helped us build strong relationships with our customers, our professional advice, timely delivery and after-sales support has made them our loyal customer for decades.

Mangla Hoists aims to ease its clients’ complex and demanding needs of projects by providing risk-free and effective lifting solutions at the most competitive prices.

Key Segments We Cater To:


#1. Energy & Power

Mangla Hoists cranes and handling equipment has been working closely with the Power industry. We customize our products to fulfill and assist the challenging requirements of this industry.

With the help of our outstanding research and development team, we identify opportunities and innovate products to solve our client’s problems. We are focused to simplify installation and maintenance of power plants. With our attention to details and after-sales service, we are trusted by industry leaders.

Mangla Hoists has supplied customized cranes & equipment for:

  • Thermal Power Plants
  • Hydro-Electric Power Plants
  • Nuclear Power Plants
  • Waste to Energy Power Plants

Conforming to stringent design and safety norms, we have achieved a high rate of repeat clients over the years, giving a boost to our business.

#2. Automobile

The **automobile industry** is highly dependent on lifting and handling equipment at every step, and Mangla Hoists has been supplying holistic solutions.

Mangla Hoists & Cranes are designed, keeping in mind the requirements of modern automobile plants and prefabricated sheds. Our superior quality, combined with prompt service & support, has helped us gain a strong foothold in this industry.

#3. Steel

The thriving **Steel industry** is a segment that has shown its faith in Mangla Hoists & Cranes for a long time now. This industry needs Extra-Heavy duty application cranes that can efficiently perform at very high temperatures and extreme conditions.

Mangla Hoists provides cranes to the **steel industry** that are high in speed but rugged in design and, above all, easy to maintain, thus ensuring safety, reliability, and uninterrupted performance.

Some special features of Mangla Hoists cranes:

  • Extra Heavy Duty Design
  • Zero Breakdown
  • Rugged
  • Easy to Operate

Mangla Hoists is acutely aware of the individualistic needs of our clients and hence, we customize our products to suit their demands. The growing number of steel clients is evidence that our products are ahead of others.

#4. Chemicals & Fertilizer

The lifting and handling requirements of the **Chemicals & Fertilizer industry** are starkly different from others and thus, catering to them is a challenge. Mangla Hoists has always taken challenges head-on and therefore, we have designed cranes and equipment that conform to the ever-demanding process application of the Chemicals & Fertilizer industry.

As safety is the utmost priority for this industry, we provide special features to **resist corrosion,** improvised by our efficient team for **long life** and to prevent any mishap.

Additionally, Mangla Hoists extends the advantage of a completely dedicated team for after-sales services that respond at the earliest. As a result, we have created a long-lasting relationship with our customers who trust our products completely.

#5. Construction & Cement

Mangla Hoists is proud to have partnered a vast number of infrastructure projects and has successfully delivered solutions to multiple industries. Following the mission and vision of our company, we understand the specific requirements of the **construction & cement industry** and hence, manufacture cranes that are specially designed for successful application in this field.

Catering to the modern construction needs Mangla Hoists gives an option of an extensive range of lifting equipment that smoothens the building of everything, be it bridges, dams, skyscrapers, et al.

By continuously innovating and improvising our products, we have been successful in capturing a wide market base with great success. Our customers come to us again and again as our products guarantee:

  • Excellent Quality
  • Safety
  • Durability
  • Longevity
  • Value for money
  • Low maintenance

#6. Oil & Gas

**Oil & Gas industry** is completely dependent on specialized lifting solutions for their varied operations and Mangla Hoists has always been a reliable partner for companies in this segment.

We have a wealth of onshore and offshore experience, fulfilling various requirements, be it for fabrication yards, decks, subsea structures, construction, extension, or maintenance and repair work.

Our specially designed cranes cater well to all application needs without compromising on performance and safety features.

#7. General Engineering & Heavy Industries

Mangla Hoists has been a frontrunner in supplying state of the art cranes and lifting equipment to the **General Engineering & Heavy Industries** sector.

We have extended our products and services to a very wide range of industries like:

  • Pumps
  • Boiler
  • Wind Turbine
  • Transformer
  • Shipbuilding
  • Generator
  • Pulverizer
  • Cement Mill
  • Pressure Vessels

Our customers come to us for repeat orders, recommend us to others as they are highly satisfied by the quality, performance, cost and service of our products.

#8. Ports & Cargo Terminal

Keeping in mind the extensive needs of different types of cranes used at **Ports,** Mangla Hoists manufactures a wide range of material handling equipment.

Making use of our state of the art manufacturing unit and cutting edge technologies, Mangla Hoists provides automated machinery that assures:

  • Higher efficiency
  • Cost-effectiveness
  • Manpower savings
  • Anti-snag system
  • High performance
  • Complete safety
  • Low environmental impact

Furthermore, Mangla Hoists is a customer-centric organization that gives priority to the requirements of customers, and thus, we customize our products as per the client’s needs.

Our prompt and ethical service has helped us forge a strong bond with all our clients, and this has led to our tremendous growth.
