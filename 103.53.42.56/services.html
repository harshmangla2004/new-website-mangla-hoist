Mangla Hoists Services

We put a premium on prompt and personalized Service!

We at Mangla Hoists are acutely aware of our responsibilities and therefore, we are always ready to walk the extra mile for our esteemed clients.

We go much beyond providing lifting and handling solutions to them and give prompt after-sales support too.

Installation & Erection of Hoist & Cranes

With a highly dedicated and experienced team of engineers and technicians, we ensure smooth **installation** and commissioning of hoists and cranes. Additionally, the performance testing and inspection of the equipment is done by our team, granting complete satisfaction to our clients.

Warranty Period Service

Our products are covered by our **warranty policy** and any defect in material, part or workmanship will be rectified free of cost. Moreover, a prompt response is provided to our clients on any product that has been purchased from us and under the **warranty period.**

Mangla Hoists & Cranes are designed, keeping in mind the requirements of modern automobile plants and prefabricated sheds. Our superior quality, combined with prompt service & support, has helped us gain a strong foothold in this industry.

Machines

We give priority to the smooth functioning of all equipment so that optimum productivity is achieved by our clients. To guarantee this, we conduct periodical health check-ups during the **warranty period.** On expiry of the warranty, Clients are advised to take **Annual Maintenance Contracts**, which will help in maintaining the machines at a nominal cost.

On-demand Service

Mangla Hoists is a customer-oriented company and in our endeavor to excel in service areas, we provide **on-demand service** and assist in repairs of machines or replacement of parts.

Spare Parts

We manufacture and supply all **spare parts** related to our machines and if any component like gearbox, wheels, shafts, rope drums, pinions, gears, wheels, etc. stops working, it can be easily replaced by us.

We always advise our customers to abstain from purchasing locally manufactured spares and instead encourage them to buy original **spare parts.** Original spares will be beneficial as it will be a perfect fit and increase the uptime of the crane/hoist, ensuring its long life.

For Service related queries, feel free to contact us.
