Ratchet Lever Hoist

A **Ratchet Lever Hoist** is also known as a chain pulling device or a ratchet puller. It is a hand-operated chain hoist for lifting or lowering heavy loads, and its practical design eliminates the need for additional machinery.

Built with German technology, it offers easy chaining when the selector lever is set to the middle position. The hook and the load chain can be pulled up or down effortlessly.

Mangla offers Indef Stier **Ratchet Lever Hoists**, as well as other economy-range hoists for less intensive applications where budget is a priority. We customize and adjust the chain height of the lift as per your requirements.

Features of Stier Ratchet Lever Hoist

  • Best German Technology: Indef uses the best German designing methodology, which has a proven record to assure reliability. All the **Ratchet Lever Hoists** offer perfectly designed housing gear, reinforced frame, and safe automatic brakes.
  • Corrosion Resistance: In order to meet the demands of certain industries like chemical, food, sewage workshops where environmental conditions are harsh, Indef manufactures **Ratchet Lever Hoists** with a galvanized load chain and tempered high tensile load chain.
  • Quality Products: Mangla does not compromise with the standards and the quality of its products. Therefore, the **Ratchet Lever Hoists** are compliant with DIN norms i.e. standard 1.5 lengths, and longer lengths as per the requirement of the client.
  • High efficiency: Indef manufacturers **Ratchet Lever Hoists** with precision, for example, Chain guide leads to load chain properly into a sprocket wheel without jamming and sticking. Furthermore, protected hand grips prevent manual slipping and hurting of fingers, double guide roller greater resistance to inclined loading.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Ratchet Lever Hoist?

    • **Lever hoists** are generally used when it is required to hoist the load upwards where the user is positioned and has access to operate the **Ratchet lever**. Similar to a Chain block being used for hoisting from ground level, a **Ratchet Lever Hoist** is commonly used to tension, pull and position loads, horizontally.

  • How to suspend a ratchet lever hoist?

    • The most common way is to use the top hook and a beam clamp or trolley. To hoist the load upwards, the clamp or trolley should be secured to a suitably rated beam and the lever hoist attached using its top hook. Then, ensure the safety catch is closed. Hoisting can be initiated by cranking the ratchet on the unit, which lifts the material as the chain passes through the ratchet hoist unit. By clicking the directional switch to the down position and continuing to crank operate the ratchet in the same way, this lowers the load.

  • What are some of the safety features of Indef Ratchet Lever Hoist?

    • It comes with a protective handgrip that prevents manual slipping, avoiding any chance of hurting fingers. Reinforced frame and safe automatic brakes make **Ratchet Lever hoist** safe and reliable. Double guide roller gives **Ratchet Lever** greater resistance making it an ideal choice for inclined loading. Spark Resistant materials are used to make it safe for explosive/mining atmospheres.

  • What is the load capacity of Indef Ratchet Lever Hoist?

    • Indef **Ratchet Lever Hoist** can easily hoist weights from the range of 0.75 Ton to 9 Tons.
